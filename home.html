Welcome to Shark's Swim Club

Welcome to Shark's Swim Club's website! Our club is dedicated to fostering competitive swimming skills and providing a supportive environment for swimmers of all ages. Our competitive swimmers are assigned to a coach who guides them in their training journey. They are divided into two age-based teams: the Youth Team for swimmers under 18 years old and the Senior Team for swimmers aged 18 and above.

Each competitive swimmer is registered based on their active swimming disciplines. Within each discipline, we continuously track and record each swimmer's best training results and the date achieved. For those swimmers who participate in competitions, we also document the event, placement, and time achieved. These individual swimmer results serve as the basis for the coach to select participants for upcoming competitions.

Our swimming disciplines include butterfly, freestyle (crawl), backstroke, and breaststroke. These disciplines are further categorized for both junior and senior swimmers. At Dolphin Swim Club, we strive to create a supportive and competitive atmosphere that encourages our swimmers to excel in their chosen disciplines. Join us and dive into the world of competitive swimming today!

Membership fees

  • For active members, the membership fee for youth swimmers (under 18 years) is 1000 DKK per year, and for senior swimmers (18 years and above) it is 1600 DKK per year.

  • Members over 60 years old receive a 25% discount on the senior fee.

  • For passive membership, the fee is 500 DKK per year.
